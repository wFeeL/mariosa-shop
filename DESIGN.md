# Mariosa Jewelry — design direction

## Thesis

Sunlit bead atelier in a precise editorial frame. Real Mariosa photography carries the color; the interface uses ink, warm milk and one mustard accent borrowed from the branded jewelry pouch.

## Dials

- Editorial luxury: 8/10
- Asymmetry: 6/10
- Information density: 4/10
- Motion: 3/10
- Warmth: 7/10

## System

- Display type: Prata. Interface and body: Manrope.
- Colors: milk `#f3efe6`, ink `#171713`, mustard `#c9a227`, sage `#6f755a`.
- Radius: restrained, from 14px to 28px. Product photography stays close to the rectangular editorial crop.
- Motion: only short opacity and transform transitions, with reduced-motion support.
- The site is browser-native. Telegram is a contact destination only.

## Content rules

- Preserve Marina's warm, personal voice and capitalized «Вы».
- Never invent stock, delivery, payment, production time, reviews or policy details.
- Both «В наличии» and «На заказ» products can be included in a request.
- Product sets are represented as separate products with their own prices.
- For the summer spider necklace without a known price, show «Цена по запросу».
